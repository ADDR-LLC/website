• Frame
    The frame used is a 3D printed quadrotor X-Frame, using ABS
    plastic as the material weighing around 500 grams.
  • ESC
    The SpeedyBee BLS 60A 30x30 4-in-1 ESC delivers a continuous
    current of 60A per channel, with a peak of 80A for 10 seconds,
    making it suitable for high-powered 6S motors.
  • Flight Controller
    The Matek H743-Wing V2 features an STM32H743VIT6 microcontroller,
    dual IMUs, a barometer, and an OSD chip. The high-precision
    current sensor handles up to 90A continuous and 220A peak currents.
    Serves as the drone's central processing unit, coordinating inputs
    and managing flight dynamics for precise control.

    The Zeee 6S LiPo Battery offers a 5200mAh capacity, features an EC5 connector
    suitable for high-performance drones and RC models. Provides high-capacity power,
    supporting extended flight times and delivering the necessary current
  • Props
    The Gemfan 7035 propellers are 7-inch, tri-blade props made from reinforced
    glass fiber nylon, designed for Cinelifter FPV and macroquad drones. Offer
    balanced thrust and efficiency, contributing to stable flight characteristics
    and responsive handling.
  • Radio Unit
    The Happymodel ExpressLRS ES900TX + ES900RX Long Range Combo helps in reliable
    long-range communication between the transmitter and receiver, maintaining
    control over greater distances.
