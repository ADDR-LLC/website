Our Mission

Drones have seen rapid adoption in industries like agriculture, videography, and logistics. We as ADDR see drones having potential in life-saving scenarios, aiming for the greatest possible impact within disaster response and search and rescue operations.

Our Vision

Leveraging AI advancements and powerful hardware, we envision a flexible network of autonomous drones working with a master AI system to rapidly access hard-to-reach areas and streamline emergency response and communication. Our ultimate goal is to reduce response times and make rescue operations more accurate and efficient, and remove the possibility of any type of error. In the future, we plan to expand into the different horizons of the drone technology.

Meet Our Team

Our team consists of dedicated professionals with expertise in AI, robotics, and emergency response. We are committed to making a difference in the world through innovative drone technology.
